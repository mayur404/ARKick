ARKick is your personal, contextual, social Augmented Reality Sidekick.

» Prime Features

ARKick is bridging your physical contextual world to your social world:

» SideKick Engine

SideKick Engine is the core of ARKick which does all the understanding and adapting for contextual mode to recommend the best possible places.

» Location

Using your GPS, SideKick Engine looks for your position on the globe.

» Weather

SideKick Engine makes sure it knows what the weather is like around you so that you don't get wet or sunburnt.

» Temperature

SideKick Engine also monitors the temperature around you, allowing it to recommend ambient places.

» Compass

Using your magnetic sensor a.k.a. the compass ARKick makes a radar for you.

» Social World

ARKick currently uses Foursquare and Facebook to bridge your social world into the real physical world.
